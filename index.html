The onslaught of weekly meal planning can deter even the most passionate of home cooks. With AutoMeals you can look foreward to never having to struggle through another weekly plan- EVER AGAIN. AutoMeals is not a recipe recommendation service an as such it will not be automated out of the box. We believe that you are the only person who can decide what you want for dinner so we will make sure AutoMeals works both for new found gems and old family staples.

There are many meal planning solutions out there; some rely on the what happens to be available while others are more of a meal planning service. No other meal planning app/service seeks to solve your planning problems for years to come. Its like this: first add your family's faves and set them to reoccur however often fancies you , then as you go add in recipes and either manually choose the frequency of recurrence (if any) or turn on automation mode. If you've never used a recipe AutoMeals in automode will ask you how dinner was and based on your responses will begin to fill in the calendar and you'll be on your way to meal planning freedom.

Will the app have a shopping list? (ingredient extraction from recipes)
Ideally AutoMeals will scrub recipes for ingredients and order them for easy shopping. This feature can be especially useful for bulk shoppers/econocooks.The categorizing features will likely be a premium feature and/or introduced in future revisions.
